WOLVES IN ROBES

Welcome, heretic or holy. Doesn’t matter which.

You stand at the threshold of judgment — not the kind handed down by cowards in robes, but by wolves who remember the cries the world forgot.

We are Wolves in Robes.
We swore no oaths. We serve no kings.
Our gospel is grit. Our scripture is suffering.
And our only choir is the howling of the broken.

Every verse you hear is a blade.
Every chorus, a reckoning.
We do not rap for fame. We rap for the fallen.

So step inside. But know this:
Once you’ve heard the Tape...
you don’t walk away clean.
