The Judgment Tape

1. Wolves Bleed Gold – Judah Kain

Intro track. Soulful brutality. Survivor's fury baptized in truth.

2. Lead Psalms – Obsidian Saint

Bulletproof gospel over choir loops. Bars made of tombstones.

3. Bulletproof Confession – Iron Shepard

Raw soul spill. No metaphors, just consequences.

4. Vendetta Season – Lil Vendetta

Young, loud, pissed-off and surgical. Dark drill energy.

5. Checkmate in Blood – Rook 7

Chessboard metaphors with sniper focus. Ruthless wordplay.

6. Hollow Gospel – Eli Drex

An ex-killer confesses everything… to the beat. Deadpan brilliance.

7. Reject the Crown – Sinai Verse

Occult bars. Sinister mysticism. Feels like a séance.

8. Flow Through the Night – Ghost Sermon

A haunted sermon whispered through the wires. Ghost Sermon flows like corrupted scripture, carried on midnight static.

9. Unstoppable – Lil Vendetta

Victory anthem — raw, proud, untamed. A firestarter.

10. Holy Rebellion – Judah Kain & Obsidian Saint

The closing ritual. Prophecy meets punishment. Two wolves, one mission.
